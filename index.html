Tap News: Real-Time News Scraping Website

Tap News is a project I build with two of my friends. This is a website which will collect daily news from CNN, BBC, ESPN... for users to read. The website will classify the category of news automatically, and thus recommend news to users. User can also adjust their preference to see the recommendation news made by the website.

Technology We Use:

  • React
  • Node.js
  • Python
  • Tensorflow

    Here's the cool thing about Fertility Rate

    1. Fertility Rate around the world is decreasing

    That is a good news to most of the developing country! With less child born, higher chance for the education and income they might receive!

    2. Fertility Rate is "Strongly" correlate to income of the country (GNI)

    With higher income of the country, the fertility rate will become lower compare to other country. Isn't it interesting?
